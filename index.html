Launching the Dynamic Product Selector

H&R Block • DIY Onboarding

Led the introduction of a dynamic product selector to improve early-funnel decision-making and reduce drop-offs during SKU selection for DIY customers.

Challenge

64% of users were dropping off during the product selector and SKU offer screens, significantly stalling funnel progression and limiting monetization opportunities at the top of the journey.

Solution Approach

  • • Evaluated a Dynamic Product Selector (DPS) against the existing 9-tile experience using retention and funnel behavior data
  • • Designed the selector to more accurately guide users to relevant SKUs early in the flow
  • • Measured impact using retention-to-start, offer visibility, and paid attachment metrics

Optimizing Upsell Recommendations

H&R Block • DIY

Designed and tested recommendation-driven upsell strategies within the product selector to improve attach rates and revenue, with a focus on post-selection nudges for Free users and prior clients.

Challenge

A significant 19% of users exited the product selector after landing on the Free SKU, resulting in missed upsell opportunities and suboptimal monetization.

Solution Approach

  • • Designed and tested a recommendation logic layer ('recommendation hat') to surface higher-value SKUs (TPR and Plus) after initial product selection
  • • Ran controlled A/B experiments to compare upsell combinations: TPR + Plus vs Free + Plus for prior clients
  • • Evaluated performance across pricing phases (Early vs Late) to understand revenue sensitivity and long-tail effects
